III. Apollo Cancer Opinion

“Apollo Cancer Opinion”(“ACO”) is an online service provided by Apollo Hospitals. The service is based on a software solution that integrates data from the AskApollo Registry and providesinformation pertaining to the clinical diagnosis and treatment planning for individuals who upload their medical reports and medical case history on the “Apollo Cancer Opinion” services website“Apollo Cancer Opinion”software solutions and service provide information pertaining to treatment plans specific to an previously diagnosed/treated and documented cases taking into account and individual’s goals of care, based on nationally and internationally standard treatment guidelines in oncology as well as review of data from oncologists affiliated with Apollo Hospitals.

The recommendations provided by “Apollo Cancer Opinion” shall not be construed for medical advice and “Apollo Cancer Opinion”does not diagnose, prescribe or treat medical conditions.Information provided by “Apollo Cancer Opinion” is purely informativeand does not replace an oncologist's or treating physician's expert judgement. Data uploaded to “Apollo Cancer Opinion” software and online service solutions will be reasonably deidentified and stored, shared, and commercialized by the “Apollo Cancer Opinion” database.

Users of “Apollo Cancer Opinion” software and online service solutionsshall evaluate the relevance and safety of the information provided by “Apollo Cancer Opinion” in consultation with his/her oncologist/ treating physician.Further the oncologist/treating physician may consider other courses of treatment not recommended by “Apollo Cancer Opinion” at their sole discretion. Effectively, “Apollo Cancer Opinion” does not have any influence or control on the treatments received by users or patients whose data was processed by “Apollo Cancer Opinion” software and online service solutions. The treating oncologist/treating physician has the ultimate authority over the medical care and treatment of all users or patients whose data was processed by “Apollo Cancer Opinion.”

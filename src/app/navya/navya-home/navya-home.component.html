The right treatment plan - is the best step to cancer cure.

Treatment opinion from the best oncologists at Apollo.

Navigating a healthcare system when diagnosed with cancer is sometimes very exhausting and overwhelming. Accepting and acknowledging the cancer diagnosis, evaluating different opinions on treatments, (radiation or chemotherapy after surgery or before surgery), meeting doctors, undergoing biopsy and PET CT and MRi can be very challenging.

Choosing the right health care facility for your treatment is a major decision.
Once you have been diagnosed, seek the best treatment plan.
And once you have been offered a treatment plan, do not hesitate to seek a second opinion. It matters.

The following considerations improve the chances of a successful treatment for cancer patients.

  1. Reputation and treatment facilities of the cancer care center. Is there a good surgical and radiation department?
  2. Expertise of the treating doctors. Are they reputed and known as the best oncologists in the country?
  3. Evidence based treatment plan. Am I receiving the right treatment based on the world's best cancer research?
  4. Comprehensive cancer center. Can I get biomarker mutation tests and take care of chemotherapy side effects?
  5. Multidisciplinary specialists and teamwork. Can my doctors and I get a second opinion from other specialists?

Apollo Hospitals brings to you a unique Online Cancer Opinion service for patients and families seeking information on cancer from specialist oncologists. You can know the best treatment for your cancer within days of diagnosis.

Through this service, patients anywhere in the world upload their medical reports and have their cases reviewed by some of the best oncologists in the world. With its large network of 71 hospitals, and 7 comprehensive cancer centers, Apollo aims to provide patients the best possible treatment plans keeping in mind the latest cancer research, and then providing cutting edge treatments at its cancer centers. Given the criticality of getting the correct treatment at the right time, which can often be stressful and time consuming, the Apollo Cancer Opinion service will simplify the process by providing detailed treatment plans to patients who then can be better prepared and informed on their treatment modality, length of stay at cancer centers, and overall prognosis.
